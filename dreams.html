One of my dreams that no one knows about is to become a professional car or motorcycle driver. Even my parents didn't know about this dream, it's the first time I've talked about it. Since I was a child I have always had a great passion for driving and beautiful cars/motorcycles, a passion that has grown with me. When I got older and could start taking my first lessons, I realized that it would not be possible to realize this secret dream because my parents would never have allowed me to do it and also many people know that it is too dangerous with high risck; furthermore only a few people manage to reach professional levels and, above all, the overall cost for this career is very high, it is estimated that you will have to spent around 2 million euros and it is impossible to start competing as payers without any help from the sponsors. For these and other reasons I have never dedicated myself to this world but this small but big dream will always remain in my heart... who knows one day I will be able to race with my friends on one of those tracks with our motorbikes/cars sports

One of my dreams that could still come true is to become a professional footballer. Since I was a child I have had this dream which, in these 15 years, I have always tried to realize. In these years I had the opportunity to express my skills in a few teams, but I also had the opportunity to play against very important Italian and foreign teams such as Sevilia. At the moment I have not yet signed any professional contract, but I have had the opportunity to do some trainigs for some teams such as Inter, Novara, Milan. As already mentioned in my hobbies, I also had the opportunity to play with the Renate first team and be called up to some matches. Unfortunately I didn't have the chance to make my debut in the Serie C or in the Italian Cup, but I was able to play in some friendly matches against some Serie C and Seria A teams such as Monza. In those moments I felt many emotions but I will never forget the satisfaction of being there and the possibility of showing my skills. Ahimè, I haven't reached this goal yet but it's still possible because I'm still young and maybe in the next few years I'll play very well enough to intrigue some important teams and be bought. My dream is to become a Juventus' footballer because it is my favourite team
